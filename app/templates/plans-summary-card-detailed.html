GOV.UK PaaS pricing

Pay for what you use above the minimum for each tier. Above the minimum fee, normal billing by the hour applies until the tier upper limits.

You can increase or decrease your compute capacity depending on the demands of your application and only pay the specified hourly rate for the instances you use.

Estimate your monthly bill using the examples below. These show the maximum you would pay using all the available plans.
