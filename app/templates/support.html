Support for GOV.UK PaaS

To find out about GOV.UK PaaS or how to use it, please send a detailed description of what you need to our support team at:

gov-uk-paas-support@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk

Starting a trial

If your team wants to start using GOV.UK PaaS, please send:

  • email addresses of all your staff who need access including at least two people who will administer the account (Org Managers)

Joining a team

If your team is already using GOV.UK PaaS and you need an account, ask an Org Manager on your team to make the request.

Your privacy

We need to store data about you to provide your account. See our privacy policy for details.
