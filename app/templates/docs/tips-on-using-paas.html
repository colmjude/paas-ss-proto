Tips on using PaaS

Configure your deployment

Use a manifest.yml file to automatically configure your app and reduce the complexity of your push command or add extra instances for resilience.

Free up space

Stop and remove your app with cf stop will cancel your running app, but it will still have an active URL (“route” in Cloud Foundry speak) and count against your quota.

To completely remove an app and any active routes run:

cf delete -r

If your still need more space contact support to talk about increasing your quota above the free tier.

Work with more spaces

Contact your org admin to create a new space. To find out who they are run:

        cf orgs
cf org-users

Take your prototype to the next level

Work with developers to try adding databases or other backing services - this can help you start investigating how GOV.UK PaaS could help you during the Beta phase of your project
